The Reality

YOUR HOME IS VULNERABLE

90% of home ignitions during wildfires are caused not by direct flame, but by ember intrusion and radiant heat through vents, eaves, and windows — the exact points that standard construction leaves unprotected.

Sprinklers fail when the grid goes down. Gel coatings require professional re-application before every fire. Ember-resistant vents are passive — they don't stop radiant heat. Nothing on the market gives you full-structure, deployable protection you can use yourself.

The System

EVERYTHING YOUR HOME NEEDS

🛡️
Aluminized Outer Shell
Multi-layer aluminized fiberglass wraps your entire structure — roof, walls, and eaves. Reflects 95%+ of incoming radiant heat before it reaches your home's surface.
⚡ Rated to 2,000°F peak exposure
🧱
Ceramic Fiber Batting
1.5" silica/ceramic fiber insulation between the outer shell and your home maintains a critical temperature differential, keeping wall surfaces well below ignition threshold.
⚡ 1,000°F sustained resistance
🔒
Magnetic Vent Seals
Ember intrusion through soffits, ridge vents, and gable ends is the #1 cause of home ignition. Magnetic snap-close covers eliminate this pathway entirely.
⚡ 100% ember intrusion block
🪟
Window Aluminized Shields
Pre-cut spring-clip aluminized panels for every window. Block radiant heat transmission through glass and prevent frame ignition. 2 minutes per window to deploy.
⚡ 2-minute deployment per window
⚓
Weighted Ground Skirt
Integrated ballast channels run the entire perimeter of the wrap at ground level. Fill with sand or water to anchor the system against 60+ mph wildfire winds.
⚡ 60 mph wind resistance
💧
Integrated Hose Ports
Four standard ¾" hose connections allow continuous surface wetting. Gravity-fed tank adapter included — works even when municipal water pressure fails.
⚡ Off-grid water compatible
Deployment

DEPLOY IN 4 STEPS

01
Receive Alert
CAL FIRE evacuation warning received. Begin deployment immediately — don't wait for flames to arrive.
0 min
02
Seal All Openings
Apply magnetic vent covers to all soffits, ridge vents, and gable vents. Clip window shields on each window.
~8 min
03
Deploy the Wrap
Pull the roof-edge rail release handle. The aluminized wrap unrolls from eave to ground on all sides.
~15 min
04
Anchor & Evacuate
Fill ground skirt ballast channels. Connect hose ports if water is available. Then get your family to safety.
~7 min
