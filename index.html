Deployment

4 Steps. One Adult. Under 30 Minutes.

01
Receive Alert
CAL FIRE evacuation warning received. Begin deployment immediately — don't wait for flames to arrive.
0 min
02
Seal All Openings
Apply magnetic vent covers to all soffits, ridge vents, and gable vents. Clip window shields on each window.
~8 min
03
Deploy the Wrap
Pull the roof-edge rail release. The aluminized wrap unrolls from eave to ground on all sides simultaneously.
~15 min
04
Anchor & Evacuate
Fill ground skirt ballast channels with sand or water. Connect hose ports if available. Then get your family to safety.
~7 min
